Tabela de Transformadas de Laplace Fundamentais

A seguir, apresentamos uma tabela com as transformadas de Laplace mais comuns, incluindo a função no domínio do tempo \( f(t) \), sua transformada \( F(s) \) e o cálculo correspondente.

Função \( f(t) \) Transformada \( F(s) \) Cálculo da Transformada
\( f(t) = 1 \) \( F(s) = \frac{1}{s} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{1\} = \int_0^\infty 1 \cdot e^{-st} \, dt = \left[ -\frac{e^{-st}}{s} \right]_0^\infty = \frac{1}{s} $$
\( f(t) = e^{at} \) \( F(s) = \frac{1}{s - a} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{e^{at}\} = \int_0^\infty e^{at} e^{-st} \, dt = \int_0^\infty e^{-(s - a)t} \, dt = \frac{1}{s - a} $$
\( f(t) = \sin(\omega t) \) \( F(s) = \frac{\omega}{s^2 + \omega^2} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{\sin(\omega t)\} = \int_0^\infty \sin(\omega t) e^{-st} \, dt = \frac{\omega}{s^2 + \omega^2} $$
\( f(t) = \cos(\omega t) \) \( F(s) = \frac{s}{s^2 + \omega^2} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{\cos(\omega t)\} = \int_0^\infty \cos(\omega t) e^{-st} \, dt = \frac{s}{s^2 + \omega^2} $$
\( f(t) = t^n \) (para \( n \geq 0 \)) \( F(s) = \frac{n!}{s^{n+1}} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{t^n\} = \int_0^\infty t^n e^{-st} \, dt = \frac{n!}{s^{n+1}} $$
\( f(t) = u(t - a) \) \( F(s) = \frac{e^{-as}}{s} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{u(t - a)\} = \int_a^\infty e^{-st} \, dt = \frac{e^{-as}}{s} $$
\( f(t) = \delta(t - a) \) \( F(s) = e^{-as} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{\delta(t - a)\} = \int_0^\infty \delta(t - a) e^{-st} \, dt = e^{-as} $$
\( f(t) = t^n e^{at} \) \( F(s) = \frac{n!}{(s - a)^{n+1}} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{t^n e^{at}\} = \int_0^\infty t^n e^{at} e^{-st} \, dt = \frac{n!}{(s - a)^{n+1}} $$
\( f(t) = e^{at} \sin(\omega t) \) \( F(s) = \frac{\omega}{(s - a)^2 + \omega^2} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{e^{at} \sin(\omega t)\} = \int_0^\infty e^{at} \sin(\omega t) e^{-st} \, dt = \frac{\omega}{(s - a)^2 + \omega^2} $$
\( f(t) = e^{at} \cos(\omega t) \) \( F(s) = \frac{s - a}{(s - a)^2 + \omega^2} \) $$ \mathcal{L}\{e^{at} \cos(\omega t)\} = \int_0^\infty e^{at} \cos(\omega t) e^{-st} \, dt = \frac{s - a}{(s - a)^2 + \omega^2} $$
